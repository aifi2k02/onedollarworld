# The 24 Launch Countries

Chosen for **contrast** (the gap is the story), **data coverage** (all appear in World
Bank / ILOSTAT / WFP datasets), and **geographic spread** (the globe must look alive).
Small enough that every number can be hand-verified before launch.

## Anchors — "your dollar is nothing" (6)
Reference points your sharers live in; Switzerland & Japan give the extreme drama.

| Country | Currency | Region |
|---|---|---|
| United States | USD | North America |
| United Kingdom | GBP | Europe |
| Germany | EUR | Europe |
| Switzerland | CHF | Europe |
| Japan | JPY | Asia |
| Australia | AUD | Oceania |

## Curation order

1. **Pakistan + USA** — already seeded as the worked example (`estimate` → replace first).
2. Remaining amplifiers (highest emotional payload, drives launch shares).
3. Anchors (needed for the contrast cards).
4. Mid tier.

A country only flips to `is_published = true` once all 10 hero-basket items are verified
to ≥ `medium` confidence.
